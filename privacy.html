Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 20, 2026

The short version

The Clocksmith app collects no data. It has no analytics, no tracking, no accounts, and it never connects to the internet.

The Clocksmith app

  • Runs entirely on your Mac and makes no network connections of any kind.
  • Reads the schedule configurations in your ~/Library/LaunchAgents folder and your Mac's local system log, solely to display them to you. This information never leaves your Mac.
  • Stores its own settings (such as your label prefix preference) locally in macOS user defaults.
  • Contains no analytics, telemetry, crash reporting, or advertising SDKs.

This website

This site is a static page hosted on GitHub Pages. It sets no cookies and runs no analytics. GitHub may collect standard server logs (such as IP addresses) as described in GitHub's privacy statement.
